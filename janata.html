MESSAGE FROM PRINCIPAL
Dear prospective students,
I would like to welcome you all to Janata School. Our mission here at Janata is and has been to provide a safe and intellectually challenging environment that will empower our students to become creative problem solvers and inspired leaders of tomorrow.
At Janata, we help students hone their academic skills and work towards providing a holistic education that values each student's individuality ultimately helping them in realizing their own potential. Students are encouraged to explore and think independently. Rote learning is discouraged and Research Based learning is promoted instead.
Janata School provides facilities for the pursuit of sports, inculcating team spirit and healthy competition. Everest gives its students ample opportunities to fulfill their academic, cultural, and social potential. It has been our effort to instill in our students, the values of trust, honesty, and service before self. We are committed to giving our society, intelligent, informed, and socially responsible citizens by imparting all-round high-quality education.
With Best Regards,
Shankar Prasad Bhandari
Principal
